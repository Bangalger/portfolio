CORRALITO RADAR

COUNTRY RISK DETECTOR DETECTOR DE RIESGO PAÍS

While watching the movie 'Plata Dulce', I was struck by the patterns I detected: repeated stories, similar phrases. So I thought it would be interesting to build a country risk detector that, based on news, indices, and other data points, could alert and relate current events to the film. I still need to validate this with a financier, so don't take what the site says seriously.
